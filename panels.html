Panel Descriptions

AoD (Attack on Dinos)

By Eden

A workshop for people interested in origami, we will be folding two to three different dinosaurs that are relatively easy to follow along. Each origami piece will take ~10-15 minutes to finish, paper will be provided.

Cosplay Armor? In MY Dorm Room?

By Dreidel Cosplay

Want to get into building armor but don't have a big workshop at your disposal? Not to fear! Join experienced foamsmith and panelist Dreidel Cosplay for a beginner-focused lesson on how to use thermoplastic to safely make cosplay armor within the constraints of a university dorm! We'll cover budgeting, tools, safety, techniques, and give you the confidence to start building today!
