About

Animefest 2022, a time and place where animanga lovers, artists, and entertainers celebrate what we love! On February 12, 2022, participants go to the Price Center at UCSD to attend discussion and workshop panels, buy the best artwork, and check out your favorite manga in our manga library! Main Stage

Exhibitor Hall

Panels

Manga Library
